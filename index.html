A UX/UI Portfolio

Hey there! Welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Elise and I’m a French (but not the rude kind) UX/UI Designer based in Barcelona.

I love music and singing… all the time… and my colleagues LOVE (well, if you ask them they might say they don’t) that I stick a different song in their head at every break, from Disney soundtrack to the Backstreet Boys.

Enthusiast about constant learning and fast-moving environments, I strive to deliver designs which are based on detecting key pain points, understanding my user and tailoring the best solution for them from 3 to 99 years old. These are not only words, the proof is here!

Dreaming is free… so I want to learn from the best and grow in this field to become the best version of myself as a UX/UI Designer. So if you are looking for an energetic UX/UI Designer to work with, don't hesitate to contact me!

In case I did not convince you yet, you can learn more about me from my background and experience.
